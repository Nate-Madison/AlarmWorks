The Pricing

Our pricing style differs from most Security Systems Services.

Instead of having a laid out plan, we offer quotes based on aspects of your home or business. We want to ensure that the most fitting security system is set up for your use.

We will consider aspects such as the number of windows and doors when forming a quote for your house. In doing this we avoid overcharging customers for their homes or businesses. Without a flat fee, we are able to work with each customer individually to decide the best option Alarm Works can offer. Installation is included in your quote and will begin promptly following your decision to allow us to protect your home.
